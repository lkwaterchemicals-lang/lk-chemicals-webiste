UNI GERMICIDE

UNIGERMICID is a complex formulation of processed Silver, a noble metal and highly concentrated Hydrogen Peroxide. The Hydrogen Peroxide component ensures a rapid germ killing effect and the silver component ensues that the effect is long-lasting. Silver is well known for its oligo – dynamic action (cold sterilization). The effect of the two components is multiplied by a special manufacturing process of UNI GERMICID giving a synergistic effect.
